The collection of 8 planets and their moons in orbit around the sun, together with smaller bodies in the form of asteroids , meteoroids, and comets.

The planets of the solar system are (in order of distance from the sun) Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune.
In simple terms, the solar system consists of the Sun and EVERYTHING THAT ORBITS or TRAVELS AROUND, THE SUN.
